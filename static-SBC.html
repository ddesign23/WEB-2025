Soulbound certificate-SBC

Soulbound Certificates are authenticity and ownership certificates on the blockchain intended for holders of valuable physical assets (art, collectibles, luxury items). SBCs allow the user to secure their asset on the blockchain, guarantee its authenticity, facilitate resale, and view their virtual collection of physical objects protected by SBCs.
